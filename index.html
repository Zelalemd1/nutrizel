About Nutrizel

Nutrizel Integrated Health and Nutrition Consulting and Therapy Center is dedicated to providing evidence-based, culturally sensitive healthcare and nutrition services to individuals and communities across Ethiopia.

Our team of certified nutritionists, dietitians, and healthcare professionals work together to deliver personalized care that respects Ethiopian culture, traditions, and food systems.
